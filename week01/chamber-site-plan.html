Fresh Bites Food Marketplace Site Plan

Overview

Purpose

The Fresh Bites Food Marketplace website serves as an online platform where customers can discover, browse, and purchase quality food products from local vendors and suppliers. The primary purpose is to provide a convenient shopping experience for food enthusiasts seeking fresh produce, specialty ingredients, prepared meals, and artisanal food products. By connecting customers directly with food vendors, the website eliminates middlemen and ensures fresher products at competitive prices. Additionally, the platform aims to support local food businesses by providing them with digital visibility and direct access to customers who value quality and authenticity in their food choices.

Scenarios

What types of food products are available on Fresh Bites, and can I filter by dietary preferences such as organic, gluten-free, or vegan options? A health-conscious shopper visits the website looking for specific dietary options. They want to easily navigate through categories and use filters to find products that match their nutritional requirements and food preferences, ensuring they can make informed purchasing decisions.

How can I place an order for fresh produce and prepared meals, and what are the delivery options available in my area? A busy professional wants to order groceries online for home delivery. They need clear information about the ordering process, delivery schedules, minimum order requirements, and available delivery zones to plan their weekly meal preparation efficiently.

Are there customer reviews and ratings for food vendors and products to help me choose quality items? A first-time customer wants reassurance about product quality before making a purchase. They seek detailed product descriptions, customer reviews, vendor ratings, and possibly photos from previous buyers to make confident choices about which vendors and products to trust.

Style Guide

Color Palette

Color Schema

Primary Color: #2E7D32 (Forest Green)

This color represents freshness and natural food. It will be used for the main navigation bar, primary buttons, and section headings to establish the website's organic and healthy food identity.

Secondary Color: #FF6F00 (Vibrant Orange)

This warm, appetizing color evokes energy and enthusiasm about food. It will be used for call-to-action buttons like "Add to Cart" and "Order Now", highlighting important elements, and price tags to draw attention to purchasing opportunities.

Accent Color: #FFF8E1 (Cream)

This soft, neutral color provides a clean background that makes food imagery pop. It will be used for page backgrounds, product cards, and content sections to create a welcoming and appetizing visual environment.

Text Color: #1B5E20 (Dark Green)

This darker shade ensures excellent readability while maintaining the natural food theme. It will be used for body text, product descriptions, and informational content throughout the website.

Typography

Heading Font: Palatino Linotype

Palatino Linotype provides an elegant, trustworthy appearance that conveys quality and tradition, perfect for a food marketplace that values authenticity.

Paragraph Font: Book Antiqua

Book Antiqua offers excellent readability for longer product descriptions and content sections, with a classic style that complements the heading font.
